# ReviewHub Community Guidelines
**Effective Date: December 30, 2025**

These Guidelines ensure a safe, respectful environment for all users, aligned with human rights (e.g., freedom of expression, non-discrimination) and Sri Lankan laws. Violations may lead to content removal, account suspension, or bans.

## 1. General Rules
- Be honest and factual in reviews.
- Respect others: No hate speech, discrimination based on race, religion, gender, ethnicity, disability, or orientation.
- No personal attacks, harassment, threats, or doxxing.
- Content must be relevant to categories (e.g., no off-topic spam).

## 2. Prohibited Content
- Illegal activities: Promoting fraud, violence, or unlawful scams.
- Infringing: No copyrighted material without permission; see DMCA Policy.
- Misleading: No fake reviews or vote manipulation.
- Sensitive: No explicit, obscene, or harmful content (e.g., revenge porn).
- Commercial: No unsolicited ads or self-promotion.

## 3. Moderation and Reporting
- Use voting to flag issues; we auto-moderate with AI and review reports.
- Report violations via the app; we investigate promptly.
- Appeals: Contact support for content removal decisions.

## 4. Human Rights Commitment
- We support free speech but balance with protections against harm. No censorship based on viewpoints unless violating rules.

Follow these for a positive community.
