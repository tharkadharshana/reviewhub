# ReviewHub DMCA Policy
**Effective Date: December 30, 2025**

ReviewHub respects intellectual property and complies with the U.S. Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and Sri Lankan copyright laws. This policy outlines how to report infringements.

## 1. Reporting Infringement
- Send notices to [Designated Agent Email/Address]. Include: (a) Signature; (b) Identification of work; (c) Infringing material location; (d) Contact info; (e) Good faith belief; (f) Accuracy statement under penalty of perjury.
- We act expeditiously to remove infringing content.

## 2. Counter-Notifications
- If your content is removed, submit a counter-notice with similar details, consenting to jurisdiction in Colombo courts.

## 3. Repeat Infringers
- Accounts with multiple violations may be terminated.

False claims may lead to liability.
